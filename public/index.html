About Me

Who I Am

My name is Leonardo Merza and I am a electrical engineer turned software developer. I began my programming skills as a network administrator designing backend software for database deployment, backup integrity, and security management. I continued my education in various fields of web development and heavily rely on Javascript and Python. Today, my skillset widely ranges from web and mobile applications, Wordpress themes, database integration, and API design.

Design Process

Each project follows a strict design process of information gathering, prototyping, testing, and deployment into a production environment all while keeping you up to date.

Design and Plan

This involves collecting data about your business model, your competitors, and what direction you want your business to grow. Mock up designs and planning strategy to ensure smooth development.

Develop and Test

Wireframes, color themes, typography, and material design for front end construction. Speed, scalability, and data integrity for the back end. Thorough testing to keep your mind at ease.

Deploy and Maintain

From staging to deployment, allow your product to be seamlessly integrated into your business. Tech support for those last minute changes to guarantee product satisfation.
